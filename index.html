About me

Who I am as a professional

I'm a third-year Commercial Economics student at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam, currently completing my internship at Stryker Orthopaedic Instruments Northern Europe — a global leader in medical technology.

Over the past six months, I've operated at the intersection of marketing strategy, market intelligence, and operational excellence. From organising the LVO Congress in Hilversum to conducting a full warehouse audit in Mechelen, and introducing Microsoft Copilot to a pan-European team — I thrive when I can combine analytical thinking with clear communication.

I bring energy, structure, and curiosity to every project. I'm not afraid to question the status quo and I'm at my best when I have ownership and autonomy over a project with real business impact.
